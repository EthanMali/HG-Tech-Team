Production Team Policy

Guidelines for Professional Conduct and Productivity

General Rules

  • All personnel on duty must refrain from using their phones unless for church purposes.
  • All personnel must not leave their duty station unless it is an emergency.
  • I expect full professionalism and no playing around.
  • All personnel should arrive at their stations at least 15 minutes before their scheduled time to ensure readiness.
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization at your station at all times.

Failure to Comply

  • First offense: You will be spoken to directly by the team leader.
  • Second offense: You will be required to meet with a higher authority.
  • Third offense: You will be removed from the production team.

Punctuality

  • All personnel must arrive on time for their shifts. Being late without prior notification will result in disciplinary action.
  • If you anticipate being late, inform the team leader as early as possible.

Respect for Equipment

  • Handle all production equipment with care. Any damage caused by neglect or improper use will result in personal responsibility for repair costs.
  • Report any malfunctions or damages immediately to the team leader or supervisor.

Team Communication

  • Clear and effective communication is expected0 at all times. If you need assistance or encounter issues, reach out to the team lead or fellow team members.
  • Use professional and respectful language in all forms of communication, including emails and messages.

Workplace Environment

  • Maintain a positive and respectful workplace culture. Bullying, harassment, or discriminatory behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Personal items should be kept to a minimum at your station to ensure a clean and professional environment.
